Our Journey

Founded in 2023 under PT Makkanya Kopi Indonesia, MKY Group is a company focused on the lifestyle sector, especially in Food & Beverage . Currently, MKY Group manages two main brands: Makkanya Coffee , a coffee shop located in Cempaka Putih, Central Jakarta , and Makkanya Express , a mobile coffee cart that has more than 155 units operating across Jakarta as of May 2025 . As the company grows, MKY Group is always looking for new ways to innovate and reach more people. We're committed to opening more Makkanya Coffee outlets and adding more Makkanya Express units in the future. We that by serving great coffee that's easy to find and offering warm service, helping to create a better lifestyle experience for people in the city.

MAKKANYA EXPRESS

Makkanya Express is a mobile coffee concept designed to reach the middle-class market and stay closer to customers. Operating with electric bikes in key Jakarta areas—especially densely populated and office districts— it offers quality coffee at accessible prices starting from Rp. 8,000. By combining affordability, mobility, and consistency, Makkanya Express brings a fresh, positive impact to Indonesia’s coffee scene.
